# Code of Conduct

### Don't be a Dick

If at anytime you choose to do something that a rational person of average intelligence could reasonably consider your actions as "Being a dick", you are in violation of this code of conduct.
<sup>[1]</sup>

---

Do: <sup>[2]</sup>

- Be empathetic
- Be welcoming
- Be kind
- Be honest
- Be supportive
- Be polite



Don't:

- Be a Dick
